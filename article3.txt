TITLE: Gaza Cease-Fire Negotiations Reach an Impasse
DATE: 2025-06-02
URL: https://www.nytimes.com/2025/06/02/world/middleeast/gaza-cease-fire-negotiations-impasse.html
---
Cease-fire negotiations between Israel and Hamas broke down amid irreconcilable positions on military withdrawal, prisoner exchanges, and postwar control of Gaza.

Mediators warned that without a breakthrough, renewed fighting was likely, as both sides hardened their positions and timelines slipped.
